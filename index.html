    Frequently Asked Questions

    How does the Spin & Win wheel work?

    Our Spin & Win wheel is a fun way to earn points that can be redeemed for rewards. Each day, you can spin the wheel once to win points ranging from 150 to 500. These points accumulate in your wallet and can be redeemed for products or converted to cash (1000 points = â‚¹1).

    How do I claim my rewards?

    To claim your rewards, go to the "Redeem Rewards" tab and select the product you want. If you have enough points, click the "Redeem" button and fill out the claim form. For cash redemption, visit the "My Wallet" tab and click "Claim via WhatsApp" to initiate the process.

    Is there a limit to how many times I can spin?

    You can spin the wheel once per day. The spin resets at midnight local time. We may occasionally offer bonus spins during special promotions.

    How does the referral program work?

    Our referral program allows you to earn 500 points for each friend who signs up using your unique referral code and completes their first spin. There's no limit to how many friends you can refer. You can find your referral code and sharing options in the "Refer & Earn" tab.

    Do my points expire?

    Points expire after 6 months of inactivity (no spins or redemptions). As long as you're actively using your account, your points will remain valid.

    Can I transfer my points to another account?

    No, points are non-transferable and can only be used by the account holder who earned them.

    Why do I need to watch an ad before spinning?

    The ads help support this free rewards program. Watching a short ad allows us to continue offering these rewards without any cost to our users.

    How long does it take to receive redeemed products?

    Physical products typically ship within 3-5 business days after claim verification. Delivery times vary based on your location but generally take 5-7 business days within India.

    What payment methods do you accept for cash redemptions?

    We process cash redemptions via UPI, Paytm, or direct bank transfer. The minimum redemption amount is â‚¹10 (10,000 points).

    I cleared my browser data and lost my points. Can I recover them?

    Points are stored in your browser's local storage. If you clear this data, we cannot recover lost points. We recommend redeeming your points regularly or contacting us to register your account if you haven't already.
